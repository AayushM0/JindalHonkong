### 4-Step Company Formation Process

* **Step 1 - Strategic Consultation:** Initial analysis for company name reservation, structure, and capital/director advisory.


* **Step 2 - Document Preparation:** Drafting of incorporation articles, statutory declarations, registry forms (NNC1/NNC1G), and initial board resolutions.


* **Step 3 - Registry Submission:** Direct electronic filing with the Companies Registry, issuing incorporation and business registration certificates in 1 to 3 business days.


* **Step 4 - Banking & Tax Setup:** Corporate bank introductions, Inland Revenue Department (IRD) setup, and ongoing compliance onboarding.
